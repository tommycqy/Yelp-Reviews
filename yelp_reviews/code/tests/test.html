    This is a tough review because some things are outside of the restaurants hands - I will say upfront that I ordered take out from Uber Eats and it was very cold but I don't want to blame slow delivery on a restaurant.     Setting: Found this place through DoorDash's Black-owned businesses section. We ordered pick-up through DoorDash. The place was hard to find because they seemed to share a space with other restaurants. The food was ready by the time we got there and it was well-packaged.

    Food: We ordered the carne asada curly fries and the SoCal burrito with chicken. We requested queso on the side too. However, we did not get any queso and the SoCal burrito had carne asada instead of chicken. The bag that the food came in was sealed so I didn't bother double checking the food before going back home, which I regretted. The fries were really good and I imagine that they would taste even better if it was fresh. The meat is decent quality as well. The burrito was really good as well. As a Californian, I would say that it is on par with the burritos in California. This is the perfect food if you are craving a burrito or something unhealthy.

    Value:
    You get a lot of food with each order. I would say it is fairly priced. My partner and I were definitely full with just two dishes.

    Service: It was frustrating to have them mess up our order including the lack of queso and the wrong meat on our burrito. Hard to know if this is DoorDash's issue or Man vs Fries's issue. I'm going to give them the benefit of the doubt though.

    Other: I'm giving them a 5 despite them messing up my order because the food was really good. Will try them again but might have to adjust my review if they keep messing up my order.
